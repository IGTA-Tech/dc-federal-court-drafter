DC District Court Formatting Rules
LCvR 7(o)(1) - Format Requirements
  • Font: Times New Roman, 12 point
  • Spacing: Double-spaced, 2 spaces between sentences
  • Margins: 1 inch minimum on all sides
  • Page Numbers: Required on all pages
LCvR 7(n)(1) - Page Limits
  • Motions/Oppositions: 45 pages maximum
  • Replies: 25 pages maximum
LCvR 5.1(b) - Caption Requirements
  • Court name
  • Party names
  • Case number with judge initials (e.g., 1:24-cv-00123-ABC)
LCvR 5.1(d) - Signature Block
  • Attorney name
  • Firm name (if applicable)
  • Address
  • Telephone
  • Email
  • DC Bar number
LCvR 5.4 - ECF Requirements
  • Text-searchable PDF required for filing
  • Electronic signature format: /s/ Attorney Name
